1. Customer PortalGoal: 
To provide an efficient and easy-to-use platform where customers can request cleaning and maintenance services through an online portal. 
Service Search & Discovery: Customers must be able to browse a service catalog and see providers nearest to their location. 

One-Click Booking: The platform shall facilitate booking a service provider via a one-click service feature. Scheduling: Customers must be able to select a specific date and time slot for their service appointment. 

Order Tracking: Customers must have the ability to check their order status and view their booking history. 

Review & Rating System: Customers shall have the feature to rate service providers and provide feedback/reviews to assist other users. 

2. Service Provider PortalGoal: 
To allow service providers to reach customers, manage work efficiently, and provide transparent services. Profile Setup: Service providers must be able to register and manage their profile details, including uploading documentation images. 

Service Listing Management: Providers must be able to add specific service items to their listing and include descriptions of the work they perform. 

Booking Management: Providers must be able to receive service requests, manage their work schedule, and update order statuses. 

Work Completion: Providers must be able to issue a receipt or slip to be signed by the customer upon completion of the work. 

Performance Monitoring: Providers should be able to view their accumulated ratings and customer feedback to maintain service quality. 

3. Admin PortalGoal: 
To manage the overall ecosystem, ensuring system security and maintaining the platform's service offerings. User Management: Admins must have the authority to manage both customer and service provider accounts. 

Service Category Management: Admins must be able to add, update, or remove service categories and individual services from the catalog. 

Pricing Oversight: Admins shall have the ability to change service prices and manage price rules within the system. 

System Reporting: Admins must be able to view all order details and customer feedback to ensure the team provides the best possible service. 

4. Cross-Portal Interactions
Notification Triggers: When a customer books a service, the request must be instantly available to the relevant service provider. 

Real-Time Status Sync: Changes in order status made by the provider (e.g., "Work is done") must be reflected immediately for the customer. 

Automated Feedback Loop: Ratings submitted by a customer must automatically update the service provider's public profile and be visible to the Admin. 

Acceptance Criteria Checklist:
[ ] Customer portal includes search, booking, scheduling, and review/rating.
[ ] Service Provider portal includes booking management and profile setup.
[] Admin portal includes user management, service management, and reporting.
[ ] Full requirements list reviewed and signed off by the team.
