Project Scope
Development of a platform-independent web application accessible via desktop and smartphones.
Registration and profile management for Customers, Service Providers, and Administrators.
A searchable service catalog featuring categories like cleaning, plumbing, painting, and electrical work.
A localized matching system to track and suggest the nearest service providers to a customer.
A feedback and rating mechanism to ensure service quality and provider trustworthiness.
